About Us

The clinic was opened in the year 2000, and since then it has been managed by one dentist only, with over a thousand customers per year.

The clinic is located at FB Harrison, Pasay City of Metro Manila, next to a small convenience store where patients can buy food and drinks while waiting for the dentist.

We provide four services: tooth restoration, extraction, prosthetic, and teeth braces. The clinic is open Monday to Saturday from 9 am to 5 pm.

Dr. Joselito A. Medrano

Joselito A. Medrano, often called Doc J, is a married dentist with 3 children and a barangay deputy in his town. He started his career as a dentist to follow his family lineage and opened the clinic in 2000.
